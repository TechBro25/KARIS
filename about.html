About Karis School

Nurturing young minds from ages 2-12 with love, care, and excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Karis School began with a simple dream: to create a nurturing environment where young children could grow, learn, and discover their unique gifts. What started as a small nursery has blossomed into a thriving nursery and primary school serving children aged 2-12.

Our founders, experienced educators and parents themselves, understood the critical importance of early childhood education. They created Karis School as a place where Christian values meet academic excellence, where every child feels loved, valued, and encouraged to reach their potential.

Today, we continue to build on that foundation, providing a safe, stimulating environment where young minds can flourish and develop the skills, character, and confidence they need for lifelong success.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality early childhood and primary education that nurtures the whole child - intellectually, spiritually, socially, and physically - in a loving Christian environment that celebrates each child's unique gifts and potential.

Our Vision

To be the leading nursery and primary school that raises confident, compassionate, and capable young learners who will make a positive impact in their families, communities, and the world.
